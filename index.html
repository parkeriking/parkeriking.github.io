Parker King

GIS Researcher · Science Communicator · Conservation Scientist

I am the Lead Graduate Research Assistant for the CONSERVE Research Group at the Alabama Water Institute and in the Department of Geography and the Environment at the University of Alabama. My work centers on science communication, tribal resilience, disaster preparedness, GIS, and research co-production with Indigenous communities. I hold an FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate and collaborate with federal agencies and tribal partners across the southeastern United States and Northeast Oklahoma.
